Cardboard Storage Boxes

Upcycle cardboard boxes into storage solutions, which can be decorated and customized!

Materials Needed

  • Cardboard box (any sturdy box, like a shipping or shoe box)
  • Scissors or a utility knife
  • Ruler (optional, for measuring)
  • Tape (duct tape, packing tape, or masking tape)

Steps

Step 1: Choose a box of your desired height, cut if needed

Step 2: Reinforce with more cardboard boxes by cutting them into equal-sized shapes

Step 3: Decorate it! The sky's the limit

Step 4: An optional choice to create divisions with more cardboard and tape

Carboard Wall Art

Use cardboard to create fun, eco-friendly art pieces that anyone can hang on their walls.

Materials Needed

  • Cardboard (any size, depending on your design)
  • Pencil and ruler (for sketching and measurements)
  • Scissors or a utility knife (be careful!)
  • Paints (acrylic or spray paint) and brushes
  • Glue (hot glue, craft glue, or double-sided tape)
  • Decorative items (optional: glitter, yarn, fabric, beads, etc.)
  • String or adhesive hooks (for hanging)

Steps

Step 1: This is where your creativity really shines!

Step 2: You can cut the cardboard into different shapes by outlining it with a pen or pencil

Step 3: Color this in and glue/tape it onto your main piece of cardboard creating a wonder piece of art!

Step 4: You can create a hung piece by making two holes on the very top of the cardboard interest it into the holes and tying the ends
