Chair to Shelf

Transform an old or broken chair into a unique and functional wall shelf. By cutting and mounting the seat or backrest, you can create a decorative and practical storage solution for books, plants, or small items. This project is an excellent way to repurpose old furniture instead of discarding it.

Materials Needed

  • An old wooden chair
  • Saw (for cutting the chair)
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint or wood stain (optional)
  • Screws and wall anchors
  • Drill
  • Measuring tape
  • Level

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Chair: Remove any upholstery or unwanted parts, leaving just the solid wood frame.

Step 2: Cut the Chair: Using a saw, cut the chair so that the seat or backrest can be mounted flat against a wall.

Step 3: Sand and Paint: Smooth out rough edges with sandpaper. If desired, paint or stain the chair to match your decor.

Step 4: Mount to the Wall: Use screws and wall anchors to securely attach the chair shelf to the wall. Ensure it is level before finalizing.

Step 5: Decorate: Place books, plants, or small decorations on your new upcycled shelf!

Dresser to Kitchen Island

Turn an old dresser into a stylish and functional kitchen island. By repurposing the drawers and adding a countertop, this upcycling project provides extra storage while giving your kitchen a unique, rustic charm.

Materials Needed
