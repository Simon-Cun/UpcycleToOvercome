Mason Jar Lanterns

Learn how to turn glass jars into lanterns or candle holders. It’s a great way to add some cozy decor to any room.

Materials Needed

  • Any size mason jar
  • Tealight candles, LED candles, or fairy lights
  • Glass paint
  • Paintbrush
  • Scissors (optional)

Steps

Step 1: Make sure to clean your mason jar(s) beforehand!

Step 2: Use paint to create designs or a frosted effect

Step 3: Let the paint dry and insert your lights inside. It's an easy and simple process, allowing your creativity to run free!

Jar Soap Dispenser

Repurpose an old mason jar as a rustic soap dispenser! (Maybe even use the previous step to make it more aesthetic!)

Materials Needed

  • A jar with a lid (like a mason jar!)
  • A soap pump (you can reuse one from an old soap dispenser or buy one)
  • Drill or nail and hammer
  • Hot glue gun
  • Liquid soap

Steps

Step 1: Wash your jar first!

Step 2: Using your drill or nail and hammer, make a hole big enough to fit your soap pump

Step 3: If the soap pump is too long you can trim it with scissors

Step 4: Secure the soap pump to the lid by gluing it to the lid (this prevents any leaks)

Step 5: Fill your jar with soap and then you’re done! You can make your new soap dispenser even better by decorating your jar
